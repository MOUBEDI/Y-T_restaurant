Our Story

Discover the passion behind Y & T Restaurant

Y & T was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to bring exceptional food and wine to the heart of Kigali. Our name represents the founders, Yves and Theo, whose passion for culinary excellence continues to inspire our team.

We take pride in our innovative approach to traditional recipes, creating dishes that are both familiar and exciting. Our menu changes seasonally to reflect the best available ingredients, with some customer favorites available year-round.

Our wine selection, curated by Gatao, features over 100 varieties from around the world, with a special focus on African vineyards. Whether you're a wine connoisseur or new to wine tasting, our staff can help you find the perfect pairing for your meal.
